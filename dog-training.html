Dog Training

Noah’s Ark knows how important it is to properly train a dog. Not only does it help to promote greater mental health in your dog, it also benefits them by giving them a desirable way to exercise, thus also increasing their physical health. Additionally, you, as the owner, can also gain quite a lot from having a trained dog. The bond between you and your dog will grow, your dog’s behavior will be excellent (thus making it easier to care for him/her), and you will be able to show all your friends all the neat tricks your furry best friend can do.

Packages Offered

We realize that training your dog alone can be a difficult process. That is why we offer Dog Training Classes at the Ark! Here, you and your dog will receive expert tips from professionals in a fun, friendly environment. We offer a variety of reward-based training classes for all skill levels. Whether you are a first-time pet owner, or a veteran, there is something for everyone here. In the table below, you will find the types of classes that we offer and what they entail.
